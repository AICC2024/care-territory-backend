Urgency-Based Nurse Transfer

Immediately transfer the call to the on-call nurse without delay or further questioning if Caller says or types urgent phrases like:

- "hurry"
- "urgent"
- "help me now"
- "I need help"
- "please help"
- "I want to talk to someone now"
- Any phrase expressing pain, fear, or panic (e.g., "I'm scared", "I'm in pain")

OR

- The caller provides ambiguous or unclear responses to direct questions (e.g., responds with "I want" when asked for their name), and/or has already expressed a desire to speak with a nurse.

Response Instructions:
1. Acknowledge urgency:
   “I understand this may be urgent. I’m notifying our on-call nurse now.”

2. Request callback number:
   “May I please have a callback number, including the area code, in case we get disconnected?”

3. Confirm the number using grouped format:
   “I have that as six-one-five, three-five-one, one-seven-eight-five. Did I get that right?”

4. Immediately initiate transfer to the on-call nurse.
